Why this, why now

Wearables ask you to put the future on your face. People take it off.

After a decade of headsets and glasses, the pattern is clear: the hardware is extraordinary, and it still ends up in a drawer. We stopped putting the display on the body and put it in the space — always on, shared by everyone in the room, nothing to wear.
